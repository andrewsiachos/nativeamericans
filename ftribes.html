Famous Native American Tribes!!

The Most famous Native American Tribes according to their population are as following:

1)Cherokee

The Cherokee Nation is the largest tribe in the United States. With more than 350.000 members the Cherokee live within the state of Oklahoma.

2)Navajo

The Navajo(pronounced Navaho in English) are Native American people of the Southwestern United States. At more than 300.000 tribal members the Navajo Nation is the second largest tribe federally regognised by the United States(the Cherokee Nation being the largest). Their reservation covers more than 27.000 square miles of land in Arizona, Utah and New Mexico.

3)Sioux

The Sioux also known as lakota, are groups of Native American tribes and First Nations peoples in North America.
~Population: 131.048

4)Chippewa

The Minnesota Chippewa Tribe (MCT) is the centralized governmental authority for six Chippewa (Ojibwe or Anishinaabe) bands in the U.S. state of Minnesota.
~Popoulation: 115.859

5)Choctaw

The Choctaw (In the Choctaw language, Chahta)[note 1] are a Native American people originally occupying what is now the Southeastern United States (modern-day Alabama, Florida, Mississippi, and Louisiana). Their Choctaw language belongs to the Muskogean language family group.
~Popoulation: 88.913

6)Apache

The Apache are a group of culturally related Native American tribes in the Southwestern United States, which include the Chiricahua, Jicarilla, Lipan, Mescalero, Salinero, Plains and Western Apache.
~Population: 64.869

7)Pueblo

The Puebloans or Pueblo peoples are Native Americans in the Southwestern United States who share common agricultural, material and religious practices.
~Population: 59.337

8)Iroquois

The Iroquois or Haudenosaunee are a historically powerful northeast Native American confederacy. They were known during the colonial years to the French as the "Iroquois League", and later as the "Iroquois Confederacy", and to the English as the "Five Nations" (before 1722), and later as the "Six Nations", comprising the Mohawk, Onondaga, Oneida, Cayuga, Seneca, and Tuscarora peoples
~Population: 48.365

9)Creek

The Muscogee, also known as the Creek and the Creek Confederacy, are a closely related group of native North American tribes or Indigenous peoples of the Southeastern Woodlands. Mvskoke is their autonym. They are originally from a single confederated native land that now comprises southern Tennessee, all of Alabama, western Georgia and part of northern Florida
~Population: 44.085

10)Blackfeet

The Blackfeet Nation also known as the Blackfeet Tribe of the Blackfeet Indian Reservation[1] is an Indian reservation and headquarters for the Siksikaitsitapi people in the United States.
~Population: 23.583
